2. What Kuurier Is

Kuurier is a privacy-focused communication platform designed for activists and communities that need secure, censorship-resistant tools. It provides encrypted messaging, community feeds, event coordination, and emergency alerts.

3. Your Identity

Your account is identified by an Ed25519 cryptographic keypair. You are responsible for keeping your private key secure. If you lose your private key, you lose access to your account — there is no recovery mechanism because we have no way to verify your identity beyond your key.

6. Trust and Moderation

Kuurier uses a web-of-trust system. Trust scores are computed from vouches by other members. Higher trust unlocks more capabilities (posting, creating events, sending SOS alerts). The community self-moderates through verification and flagging.

Because messages are end-to-end encrypted, we cannot moderate message content. Community moderation happens at the organizational and channel level through admins and moderators chosen by the community.

7. Encryption and Privacy

All direct and group messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Signal Protocol. We cannot read, scan, or moderate message content. This is by design. See our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.
